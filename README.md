🚀 1U CubeSat Chassis Design
📌 Overview

This project presents the design of a robust and compact 1U CubeSat chassis that adheres to standard CubeSat specifications. The design focuses on structural integrity, modularity, and efficient accommodation of internal subsystems while ensuring compatibility with deployer mechanisms.

🎯 Design Objectives
Meet 1U CubeSat dimensional standards
Ensure high structural strength for launch conditions
Provide modular architecture for easy integration
Optimize internal space utilization
Support solar panel integration and future extensions
🛠️ Material Selection

The chassis is designed using Aluminum Alloy, chosen for:

High strength-to-weight ratio
Excellent corrosion resistance
Good machinability
Effective thermal conductivity for passive cooling
⚙️ Key Design Features
📦 External Dimensions
100 mm × 100 mm × 113.5 mm (Standard 1U CubeSat size)
🧱 Structural Components
Rails
Width: 8.5 mm (±0.1 mm tolerance)
Protrusion: 6.5 mm
Continuous and hole-free for deployer compatibility
Wall Thickness
Minimum: 1.5 mm
Optimized for strength and weight balance
🔌 Functional Design
Panel Slots
8 slots (2 on each ±X and ±Y face)
Width: 2.5 mm
Depth: ≥10 mm
For solar panel mounting
Cable Channel
Central cylindrical channel
Diameter: 8 mm
Enables efficient wiring and subsystem connectivity
Payload Bay
Minimum internal volume:
80 mm × 80 mm × 60 mm
Designed for electronics and payload integration
Fastening System
M3 threaded holes on top & bottom corners
Ensures secure assembly and modularity
🧪 Structural Feasibility
Designed to withstand launch vibrations and shock loads
Aluminum structure ensures rigidity and durability
Continuous rails improve load distribution
Symmetrical geometry ensures balanced stress handling
Minimizes stress concentration points
📊 Advantages

✔ Lightweight yet strong
✔ Modular and scalable design
✔ Compatible with standard deployers
✔ Efficient internal space usage
✔ Easy integration of subsystems
